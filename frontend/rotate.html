Image Rotation Augmentation

Description


We apply image rotation augmentation to enhance the performance of the LayoutLMv3 pretraining model. Rotating the image creates distinct data, improving model training. We select rotation angles from -30 to 30 degrees for each image.

For the current existing rotation augmentation technique on the market, certain portions of the image may become truncated as a result of the constraints imposed by the frame.

Hence, we introduce an improved version of this technique by conducting calculations to expand the frame to accommodate the newly rotated image, thereby enabling the complete visibility of the entire document. The frame will be appropriately modified to accommodate this.
